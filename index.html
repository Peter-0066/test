1. Economy Class: • Description: This is the most common and least expensive class. It is typically used by travelers looking for budget options. • Advantages: • Low ticket prices. • Flexible booking options. • Access to basic services like snacks and drinks (which may be paid in some airlines). • Comfortable seats, but legroom may be limited.

****

2. Business Class: • Description: Business class is a premium option aimed at travelers who need comfort and additional services. • Advantages: • Wider seats with more legroom, often converting into flat beds on long flights. • Gourmet dining and complimentary drinks. • Priority check-in and boarding. • Access to airport lounges.

*****

3. First Class: • Description:offering the highest level of comfort and service. • Advantages: • Extremely luxurious seats with ample space, often including full beds. • High-level personalized service, such as private chefs and customized meals. • Top priority for all services, including check-in and boarding. • Access to exclusive luxury lounges at airports.
